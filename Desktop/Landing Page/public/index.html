OUR MISSION

Lumina aims to reduce negativity biases and cognitive distortions among Gen Z youth in the United States. By providing journaling tools powered by AI, we aim to empower them.


Extensive studies have consistently demonstrated that negative thoughts lead to a significant risk for anxiety and depression, negatively shaping their decision and thought processes. Our objective is to alleviate the mental health challenges faced by youth, preventing the detrimental impact on their self-confidence. We firmly hope to provide Gen Z with the tools to create positivity in the world.

FEATURES

AI JOURNALING

The use of AI in a journaling space allows for LUMINA to be able to personalize a user's experience. The AI will take a user's journal to be able to prompt more in depth questions allowing for user's to be able to dive deep into their thoughts.

MEMORIES: STAR JAR

The STAR JAR is a mood tracker in which allows users to track their emotions throughout the month.

MEMORIES: VISION BOARD

The VISION BOARD allows for user's to have a keeping of their happy memories to look back on.
